Technology in Chemical Engineering

Chemical engineering is a type of science that uses properties, elements, chemicals etc to experiment with. In chemistry some of the things that are created are cures for things like illnesses or diseases, or artificially create things and materials that wouldn’t be natural. Chemical engineering heavily relies on technology which means it’s important that we have access to technology that can be used to create things that can benefit the world.

Existing Technology

Metal Analyzers (Thermo Niton XRF)

This gadget is like a gun that is used to scan materials like metals to analyze what kind of metal they are and etc with a screen that gives results on the analysis.
